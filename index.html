Gather + Graze

Charcuterie 101

What Youâ€™ll Need: A wooden cutting board or serving tray, Small bowls, Ramekin, Mini tongs, Mini wooden spoons, Tasting spoons, Cheese knives, Pedestal trays (for height)

Step 1: TOOLS + UTENSILS

Place your plates, ramekins, pedestal trays, Bowls, serving spoons, jam jars (olives, hummus, honey, mascarpone, cornichons, spreads and dips). Add your utensils (cheese knives, forks, tongs, spoons, honey dipper, toothpicks) Add any labels/signage where needed.

Step 2: CHEESES + MEATS

Choose 3 - 4 cheeses (soft, hard, semi-hard, spreadable) burrata, mascarpone, havarti, gruyere, manchego, gouda. Choose 3 - 4 meats like prosciutto, salami, pate, soppressata, or mortadella.

Step 3: VEGGIES + FRUITS

Add fresh fruits like apples, pears, Grapes, blackberries, and figs. Add in fresh veggies like tomatoes, cucumber slices, broccoli,and baby carrots

Step 4: BREADS + SWEETS

Arrange crackers, pretzels, pita chips, crispbreads, and breadsticks Add in more sweets like dark chocolate, dried fruit, jams, honey

Step 5: DECORATE + GARNISH

In the remaining spaces add in your smaller items like nuts (cashews, marcona almonds, walnuts, pecans) and then add garnishes (sprigs of Rosemary, basil, or parsley) You can also add inedible decor like flowers or greenery.

SERVE + ENJOY!

Before serving, step back and adjust. Make sure everything feels balanced (messy-but-intentional is the goal.)
